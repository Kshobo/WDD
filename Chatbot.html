Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a job?
Click "Add Job" on the main dashboard.
How does the dashboard work?
Your applications are sorted into Applied, Interview, Offer, Rejected.
How do I get notifications?
Enable job notifications in your profile settings.
How do I connect to the Adzuna job API?
You need your app_id and app_key, then the backend fetches job listings.
